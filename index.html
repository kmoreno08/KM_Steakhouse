Your Organic eatery has arrived

K&M Steakhouse is a new take to Martian food, brimming with healthful recipes, but there's nothing secret about the way we prepare our dishes. Our unique experience brings guests closer to our culinary craft and behind our meats is a selection of the prime seats available, grass-fed and organic produce that you can enjoy with a unique Wine from Mars. We take great pride in a menu that caters to nearly every food preference with a variety of Beef, Lamb, Port and Chicken selection. Our Extensive Salad bar has a gluten-free, naturally organic, vegetarian and vegan offerings. Our commitment to quality and creativity ensures a menu that encourages exploration all throughout the year.
